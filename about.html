🇸🇱 What is SaloneHub?

SaloneHub is a free website built specifically for Sierra Leoneans — whether you live in Sierra Leone or abroad. Think of it as your personal Sierra Leonean daily briefing page.

Every time you open it, you instantly see the live dollar to leone rate, current fuel prices, today's Sierra Leonean news, weather across all provinces, football scores, and much more — without signing up, downloading an app, or paying anything.

It was built because Sierra Leoneans have to visit multiple different websites just to get basic daily information. SaloneHub puts it all in one place, for free, forever.

⚙️ How does it work?
Simple — just open and use.
1

Open the website on your phone or computer

Go to sierraleonehub.com on any browser — Chrome, Safari, Firefox. Works on all phones and computers. No app download needed.

2

Your daily briefing loads instantly

At the top you see a scrolling ticker with live exchange rates — dollar, pound, euro, dirham and more. Below is a banner showing today's dollar rate, pound rate, Freetown weather and Bitcoin price at a glance.

3

Tap the tabs to find what you need

The site is organised into 6 main categories: Money, News & Info, Life & Services, Work & Travel, Entertainment, and AI Chat. Tap Money to see exchange rates, fuel prices, tax calculator, money transfer comparison and more.

4

Everything updates automatically

Exchange rates update daily from BSL, news updates every visit, weather updates live, and football scores refresh automatically. You always see the latest information.

5

Save it to your home screen for one-tap access

On Android: tap ⋮ menu → "Add to Home screen". On iPhone: tap the Share button → "Add to Home Screen". After that, tap the SaloneHub icon on your phone like any app — completely free.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions
Quick answers to common questions.
Is SaloneHub really free?
Yes, 100% free. No paid plan, no subscription, no hidden charges. Every feature on the site is free to use forever.
Do I need to sign up or create an account?
No. You do not need to sign up, create an account, or give any personal information. Just open the website and start using it.
How accurate are the exchange rates?
The rates shown are BSL (Bank of Sierra Leone) indicative cross-rates calculated from international forex data. They are updated daily. Always verify with your bank before any financial transaction. The New Leone (SLE) rate is clearly shown — note that 1 SLE = 1,000 old Leones (SLL) following Sierra Leone's 2022 redenomination.
How do I add it to my phone like an app?
On Android: Open Chrome, go to sierraleonehub.com, tap the ⋮ menu, tap "Add to Home screen". On iPhone: Open Safari, tap the Share button, tap "Add to Home Screen". You will then see a SaloneHub icon on your home screen — tap it to open instantly, just like any app. It is free and takes less than 30 seconds.
Who built SaloneHub?
SaloneHub was built to give Sierra Leoneans — at home and abroad — one free place to get all the daily information they need. It started as a personal project and grew into a full platform. It is continuously improved based on user feedback.
How do I report a problem or suggest a feature?
Use the AI Chat tab on the site and type "I want to report a problem" or "I suggest adding..." — or reach us on Twitter/X at @SaloneHubLive.
Is my data private?
Yes. SaloneHub does not collect personal data. Your preferences (like your province or favourite currency) are saved only on your own device — never sent to any server. Only general page visit analytics (Google Analytics) are used, with no personal information collected.
