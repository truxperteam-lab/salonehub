⚖️ Sierra Leone Laws & Your Rights
Key laws every Sierra Leonean should know
Data Protection

🔒 Data Protection Act 2022

  • Right to access, correct and delete your personal data
  • Businesses must register with the Data Protection Commission
  • Consent required before collecting personal data
  • Regulator: Data Protection Commission of Sierra Leone
Cybercrime

🌐 Cybercrime Act 2021

  • Online fraud, hacking and identity theft are criminal offences
  • Cyberstalking and online harassment carry prison sentences
  • Spreading false information online can be prosecuted
  • Penalties: up to 10 years imprisonment for serious offences
Labour

👷 Employer & Employee Relations Act

  • National Minimum Wage: Le 800,000/month (2024)
  • Max 8 hours/day, 40 hours/week standard
  • Maternity leave: 12 weeks with pay
  • Annual leave: minimum 21 working days
Tax

🧾 Tax Laws — Key Points

  • GST (Goods & Services Tax): 15% standard rate
  • Corporate tax: 30% standard rate
  • PAYE: progressive rates — consult NRA for brackets
  • Authority: National Revenue Authority (NRA)
nra.gov.sl →
Website Compliance

📋 What Every Sierra Leonean Website Must Have

  • ✅ Privacy Policy — explains data collection & use
  • ✅ Terms & Conditions — rules for using your site
  • ✅ Disclaimer — especially for financial or medical info
  • ✅ Contact details — physical address or email
  • ✅ If collecting payments: use BSL-licensed payment gateways

🦁 About Sierra Leone

  • Name means "Lion Mountains" in Portuguese
  • Home to the deepest natural harbour in Africa (Freetown)
  • One of the world's top diamond producers
  • UNESCO World Heritage Site: Bunce Island (slave fort)

🇺🇸 Sierra Leoneans in the USA

Returning Home

🏡 Planning to Return to Sierra Leone?

  • NaCSA (National Commission for Social Action) supports returnees
  • Bring valid SL passport or NCP (National Civil Registration)
  • Customs allowances: personal effects duty-free up to a limit
  • Contact NASSIT for pension enquiries: nassit.gov.sl →

🎵 Salone Music & Culture
Sierra Leonean artists, genres and culture
Music

🎤 Sierra Leonean Music Scene

  • Afrobeats SL: Blending Krio lyrics with Afrobeats rhythms
  • Notable Artists: Drizilik, DRRTYWULVZ, Emmerson, Star Zee
  • Popular Genres: Afrobeats, Salone music, Hip-hop Krio, Highlife
  • Annual Events: Sierra Leone Music Awards, Freetown Music Festival
Culture & Food

🌴 Salone Culture Highlights

  • Bundu / Sande Society: Female initiation society (UNESCO heritage)
  • Poro Society: Male initiation society
  • National Dish: Plasas (cassava leaf stew with rice)
  • Krio Language: English-based creole spoken widely in Freetown
  • Oktoba Fest: Annual Freetown cultural festival
Tourism

🏖️ Top Sierra Leone Tourism Spots

  • 🏖️ Lumley Beach, Freetown
  • 🌊 Tokeh Beach & Bureh Beach
  • 🐠 Banana Islands — snorkelling paradise
  • 🦁 Outamba-Kilimi National Park
  • ⛵ Tacugama Chimpanzee Sanctuary
  • 🏛️ Bunce Island Historic Slave Fort
